press ahead with 推进;加紧

voluntary license 自愿许可

compulsory license 强制许可

intellectual property 知识产权

lag behind 落后;逾期付款

dance to sb’s tune 完全照办

work like a charm 富有成效

point fingers at 指责

a community of life 生命共同体

phase v.分阶段计划
